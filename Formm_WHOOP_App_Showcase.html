What WHOOP gives you — and the gap Formm fills

WHOOP provides excellent physiological monitoring. What it cannot do is connect that physiology to your movement quality. That's the gap Formm fills — and it changes what every number means.

⌚ What WHOOP provides (validated)
HRV (RMSSD) — nightly, CCC 0.94 vs ECG
Resting heart rate — daily average
Respiratory rate — nocturnal average
Skin temperature deviation — 90-night baseline
Sleep stages — light, deep/SWS, REM, awake
Strain score 0–21 — proprietary, unvalidated vs lab
Recovery score 0–100% — proprietary, unvalidated vs lab
* Strain + Recovery showed no significant correlation with lab metabolic measures in the 2024 Lundstrom et al. peer-reviewed study
🧠 What WHOOP cannot infer (Formm fills this)
Whether form degradation is fatigue or technique
Quality-adjusted load (joint stress vs cardiovascular stress)
Whether tonight's sleep will invalidate tomorrow's form score
Three-signal convergence alert (HRV + ACWR + form quality)
Strain efficiency decoupling (body working harder per unit output)
Cross-domain asymmetry (gym mechanics + physiological suppression)
Chronotype-aligned peak training window

⚠ MANDATORY ALERT — non-dismissable
Three conditions must ALL fire simultaneously
1. WHOOP HRV z-score < −1.5 SD from personal baseline
2. Formm ACWR > 1.5 (load spike into danger zone)
3. Form score declined > 10% over last 3 sessions

Without all three: no mandatory alert fires. Two of three = individual amber cautions at most. The convergence is the signal.
WHOOP HRV (raw signal)Formm ACWR + form AI
② Why WHOOP alone can't do this
Missing both ACWR and biomechanics
WHOOP's Recovery score would show "red" when HRV is suppressed — but it cannot detect ACWR spiking (requires session load history) or form quality declining (requires movement analysis). The three-signal convergence is a Formm-exclusive inference by definition.
③ Deduplication and acknowledgement
Max one mandatory per 48h · requires tap to clear
The alert must be acknowledged with an explicit tap before the user can interact with the rest of the app. Stores dismissal in DB. Does not re-fire for 48h. FCM push notification with haptic heavy impact when generated. Never surface this without all three conditions confirmed.

③ Privacy notice — required
DPDP Act 2023 compliance (India)
India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act classifies biometric data (HRV, sleep, heart rate) as sensitive personal data. Explicit consent required before collection. "Delete all WHOOP data" must take immediate effect — not a 30-day delay. This notice must always be visible on the hub screen.
