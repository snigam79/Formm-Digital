RPE logging
The entire load management layer depends on this one data point. RPE × session duration = session load in Arbitrary Units. Under 10 seconds. One tap.
The Foster formula: Session load (AU) = RPE (1–10) × session duration (minutes). Published in Medicine & Science in Sports & Exercise (2001). The simplest validated load quantification method that requires no wearable device — just subjective effort and a clock.
